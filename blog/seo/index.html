What Is SEO and Why Your Business Needs It

By El Variente Team • May 5, 2025 • ~5 min read

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving your website's visibility on search engines like Google. If you're running a business in Sri Lanka or targeting global clients, SEO is essential for driving organic traffic, increasing brand awareness, and generating leads.

What Does SEO Include?

1. Keyword Research

SEO starts with identifying the right keywords your audience is searching for. Examples include:

  • SEO Sri Lanka
  • branding agency in Sri Lanka
  • digital marketing services
  • web development company
  • AI marketing solutions

2. On-Page SEO

This involves optimizing your website's content and structure:

  • Title tags and meta descriptions
  • Header tags (

    ,

    )

  • Image alt text
  • Internal linking
  • Mobile responsiveness

3. Technical SEO

Search engines prioritize websites that load fast and are easy to crawl. Key elements:

  • XML sitemap
  • Robots.txt file
  • HTTPS security
  • Schema markup
  • Canonical URLs

4. Content Creation

Publishing high-quality blog posts, service pages, and landing pages helps you rank for long-tail keywords like:

  • how to improve SEO for small business
  • best SEO tools for startups
  • SEO tips for e-commerce websites

5. Link Building

Backlinks from reputable websites improve your domain authority. You can earn links through:

  • Guest blogging
  • Business directories
  • Social media profiles
  • Press mentions
